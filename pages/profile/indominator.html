Information

A legendary Event hybrid, the Indominator is a highly sought-after creature, appearing only during special in-game events. It was engineered to be a perfect predator, combining the traits of several powerful carnivores. Its signature ability, Primal Cloak, allows it to vanish from sight while empowering its offensive and defensive capabilities, making it a terrifying opponent in any battle.
